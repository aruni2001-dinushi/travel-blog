My Travel Story

Hello! I'm Aruni Dinushika, a university student with a passion for exploring new places while balancing my academic life. My journey into travel began when I started taking short trips during semester breaks to destress and experience different cultures.

As a student, I've learned to travel on a budget while still having meaningful experiences. I believe that travel is one of the best forms of education - it teaches you about history, culture, and most importantly, about yourself. My goal is to visit at least one new place every semester break.

Through this blog, I want to share my experiences as a student traveler - showing that you don't need a big budget to explore the world. I focus on affordable travel options, student discounts, and meaningful experiences that enrich both my personal growth and academic perspective.
