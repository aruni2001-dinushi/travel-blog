My Travel Destinations

Ella, Sri Lanka

Ella is a small town in the Badulla District of Sri Lanka, surrounded by hills covered with cloud forests and tea plantations. It's a perfect destination for students looking for an affordable yet breathtaking travel experience close to nature.

Highlights & Activities:

  • Hike to the top of Little Adam's Peak for stunning views
  • Walk across the famous Nine Arch Bridge
  • Visit Ravana Falls and take a refreshing dip
  • Explore tea plantations and learn about tea production
  • Enjoy budget-friendly local cuisine
  • Watch sunrise from Ella Rock
Student Tip: Travel by train from Colombo to Ella for one of the most scenic railway journeys in the world at a student-discounted fare.

Kandy, Sri Lanka

Kandy is a major city in central Sri Lanka, known for its rich history, cultural significance, and beautiful landscapes. It's home to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, making it a perfect educational destination for students.

Highlights & Activities:

  • Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
  • Explore the Royal Botanical Gardens in Peradeniya
  • Watch traditional Kandyan dance performances
  • Walk around Kandy Lake in the evening
  • Visit the Ceylon Tea Museum
  • Explore Udawatta Kele Sanctuary
Student Tip: Many cultural sites offer discounted entry fees for students with valid university ID cards.

Galle, Sri Lanka

Galle is a city on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka, known for its fortified old city founded by Portuguese colonists in the 16th century. The Galle Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offers a unique blend of European architecture and South Asian traditions.

Highlights & Activities:

  • Explore the historic Galle Fort
  • Visit the Galle Lighthouse and Maritime Museum
  • Relax on Unawatuna Beach
  • Shop for souvenirs at the Galle Fort shopping streets
  • Watch sunset from the fort walls
  • Try fresh seafood at beachside restaurants
Student Tip: Visit during the off-peak season (May-November) for better deals on accommodation and fewer crowds.
